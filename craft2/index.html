About Us
CRAFT^2 is founded in April 2024. From various disciplines, my peers and I decided to establish a lab to participate in academic activities. We come from wide ranged engineering backgrounds, including mechatronics, fabrication, software development, computational algorithms, human subject studies, and design.

We share a common interest in the HCI venue, meanwhile HCI is the place that also welcomes us to integrate our interdisciplinary skills and bring together insightful projects into diverse themes.
Meet us through our selected project presentation!
